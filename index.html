LP – Local Printing

Affordable Local 3D Printing – Pay at Doorstep & Preview Before You Buy

How It Works

Send your 3D file to our email as a .3MF file. We print your model and deliver it locally. You preview the finished print at your doorstep before paying. Payment is made at delivery.

Pricing

  • $0.15 per gram of filament used
  • Minimum order: $5
  • Basic model design service: +$5 (simple shapes and basic models only)

Complex engineering designs, moving mechanisms, or advanced models may be declined. If a model is too difficult or unsuitable for printing, you will be informed before any work begins.

If a Print Fails

3D printing can sometimes fail due to design errors or technical limits. If the print fails due to a machine issue, it will be reprinted at no extra cost. If the file itself causes repeated failures, we will contact you to adjust the design.

Rules & File Requirements

  • Files must be submitted as .3MF format
  • No weapons, guns, knives, or harmful objects
  • Local area delivery only
