How It Works

Email your design as a .3MF file. Your model is printed and delivered locally. You preview it at your doorstep before paying. Payment is made at delivery.

Most prints are completed within 1–3 days depending on size and complexity.

Pricing

  • $0.15 per gram of filament used
  • Minimum order: $5
  • Basic model design service: +$5 (simple shapes only)

Complex mechanical designs, advanced engineering models, or unsuitable prints may be declined. You will always be informed before printing begins.

Materials & Colors

Printed using high-quality PLA filament. Ask about available colors in your email when sending your file.

If a Print Fails

If a print fails due to a machine issue, it will be reprinted at no extra cost. If the file design causes repeated failures, you will be contacted before continuing.

Rules & File Requirements

  • .3MF files only
  • No guns, knives, weapons, or harmful objects
  • Local delivery only
  • Once purchased, the physical printed item becomes your property. Digital design rights remain with the original creator unless agreed otherwise.

Why LP?

  • Careful quality checks before delivery
  • Direct communication — no middleman
